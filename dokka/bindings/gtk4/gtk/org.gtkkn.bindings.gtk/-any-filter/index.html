AnyFilter

open class AnyFilter(pointer: CPointer<GtkAnyFilter>) : MultiFilter

GtkAnyFilter matches an item when at least one of its filters matches.

To add filters to a GtkAnyFilter, use method@Gtk.MultiFilter.append.
